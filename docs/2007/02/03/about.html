About

03 February 2007

My name is Antoine Toulme. Welcome to Lunar Ocean.

I am the project lead of the BPMN modeler hosted by the SOA Tools Platform. I also am a committer on the Babel project.

I used to be a committer on the EMF Compare Component, which is hosted by the EMF Technology project, I made the initial contribution but didn’t follow through.

On the side, I develop a requirements tracker named Recker and a feeds funnel Funnel, all ruby-based.

Disclaimer: I work for Intalio and will only talk about Intalio when talking about BPM or BPMN, just like my colleague here. The opinions expressed in this blog only reflects my own. My employer is blogging here if you want to know more.

Feel free to contact me.

This blog is named Lunar Ocean after one of my favorite places, a coffee shop on Miramar Beach in Half Moon Bay, CA.


The catchy sentence “Always one character away from perfetcion” only really means that I am a typo away from achieving my goals.
